Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Solus is built on the belief that an app asking only one thing of you should ask nothing else. That extends to your data. Solus is designed to collect as little as possible, keep everything on your device, and never use your information for anything beyond making the app work.

This policy explains what Solus stores, why, and what your rights are. It is written to be read, not avoided.

What Solus stores on your device

Solus stores the following information locally on your device. Nothing in this list is transmitted to Solus or any third party:

  • Your daily intention text
  • The date your intention was set
  • Whether your intention has been marked as done
  • Whether you have completed onboarding
  • Your reflection text, if you choose to write one
  • Your intention journal entries and reflections over time
  • Your in-app purchase status
  • Your notification preferences and chosen reminder time
  • Your colour theme preference

All of this information is stored using Apple's standard on-device storage systems. It exists solely to make the app function correctly and to preserve your experience between sessions.

iCloud sync

From a future version of Solus, Supporter users will have the option to sync their intention history and journal entries across their Apple devices using iCloud. When this feature is active:

  • Data is stored in your personal iCloud account, managed entirely by Apple
  • Solus does not operate any servers and never receives your data directly
  • Syncing is opt-in and can be disabled at any time in the app's settings
  • Free users are not affected — their data remains on-device only

Apple's privacy policy governs any data stored in iCloud and can be found at apple.com/legal/privacy.

In-app purchases

Solus offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely through Apple's StoreKit framework. Solus does not see, handle, or store any payment or billing information. All transactions are managed directly by Apple. Apple's privacy policy applies to any information collected during the purchase process.

What Solus does not do

  • Solus does not collect personal information
  • Solus does not transmit data to external servers
  • Solus does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs
  • Solus does not require an account or login of any kind
  • Solus does not share your information with third parties
  • Solus does not sell your data, ever, to anyone
  • Solus does not display advertisements, and never will

Your rights

  • Access — you can view all data Solus holds about you at any time within the app itself
  • Correction — you can edit or update your intention and reflection content directly in the app
  • Deletion — you can delete all data Solus holds by using Reset Solus completely in Settings, or by deleting the app from your device
  • Portability — your data is stored on your own device and iCloud account, under your own control
  • No sale — your data is never sold or shared for commercial purposes

If you have a request regarding your data that cannot be resolved through the app itself, contact us at solusworkssg@gmail.com.

Users in the European Union

If you are located in the European Union, the following applies under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

The legal basis for storing your data locally on your device is legitimate interest — specifically, to provide the core functionality of the app you have chosen to download and use. No personal data is processed beyond what is necessary for the app to function. You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your rights have not been respected.

Users in California

If you are a California resident, the following applies under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

Solus does not sell personal information. Solus does not share personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioural advertising. You have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion, and to not be discriminated against for exercising your rights.

Children's privacy

Solus does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store and contains no features that target or are designed for children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

Changes to this policy

If Solus's data practices change in a future version, this policy will be updated before that version ships. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or the App Store update notes. The date at the top of this page will always reflect when it was last revised.

Your continued use of Solus after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Governing law

This privacy policy is governed by the laws of Singapore. For users in other jurisdictions, locally applicable privacy laws — including GDPR for EU residents and CCPA for California residents — apply where relevant and take precedence where required by law.

Contact

For any questions, concerns, or data requests related to this privacy policy, please contact us at solusworkssg@gmail.com. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 30 days.
