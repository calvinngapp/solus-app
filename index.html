Solus is a single-purpose iOS app. Set one intention each morning. Live with it. Mark it done. No streaks, no scores, no pressure. Just one thing, held quietly for one day.

Privacy Policy

Solus stores your intention and app state locally on your device using iOS standard storage. No data is collected, transmitted, or shared with any third party.

Solus contains no third-party SDKs, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. Your intentions are private and remain entirely on your device.

This policy applies to Solus version 1.0 and will be updated if data practices change in future versions.
